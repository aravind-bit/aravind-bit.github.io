ENGINEERING APPROACH
  • 01 Start with the real workflow: who uses the data, what decisions they need to make, and how success is measured.
  • 02 Trace the signal: map sources, transformations, metrics, and failure modes before building anything complicated.
  • 03 Ship something real: small, usable slices that solve an actual workflow rather than a theoretical one.
  • 04 Tight feedback loops: refine the system, improve trust, and keep documentation, monitoring, and stakeholder usability as strong as the build itself.
